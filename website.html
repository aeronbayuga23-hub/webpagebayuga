Senior High School Strands

STEM

Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) strand focuses on advanced concepts in science and math, preparing students for careers in engineering, health, research, and technology-related fields.

ABM

Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM) strand is designed for students who want to pursue business-related courses, including entrepreneurship, finance, and marketing, developing analytical and managerial skills.

HUMSS

Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS) strand prepares students for education, communication, psychology, and law careers. Focuses on critical thinking, writing, research, and social awareness.

TVL - Technical Vocational Livelihood

The TVL strand equips students with practical skills for immediate employment or entrepreneurship in technical-vocational fields. It has several specializations:

  • Information and Communications Technology (ICT): Develops digital skills including computer programming, web design, software development, animation, networking, and technical drafting. Students learn to create apps, websites, and multimedia projects.
  • Home Economics (HE): Prepares students for hospitality, food service, and tourism industries. Covers bread and pastry production, food and beverage services, housekeeping, and practical skills for hotels, restaurants, and catering businesses.
  • Shielded Metal Arc Welding (SMAW): Focuses on welding and metal fabrication skills. Students learn welding techniques, safety procedures, metal cutting, assembly, and quality control, preparing them for careers in manufacturing, construction, or engineering workshops.

Mission & Vision

Mission

To protect and promote the right of every learner to quality, equitable, culture-based, and complete basic education.

Vision

To produce Filipinos who passionately love their country and whose values and competencies enable them to realize their full potential and contribute meaningfully to building the nation.
